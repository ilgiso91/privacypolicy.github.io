Privacy Policy - Safa

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Safa ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Safa mobile application (the "App"). Safa is operated by Ismail Turanli. You can contact us at ismailturanli91@gmail.com.

Safa is an Islamic companion app that may provide prayer times, Qibla direction, Quran reading progress, dhikr tools, Ramadan guidance, Jumuah preparation, Yasin Sharif, Asma ul Husna, prayer guidance, nearby mosque information, notifications, and AI-assisted prompts.

By downloading, installing, accessing, or using Safa, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Support communications: If you contact us, we may receive your email address, message content, and any information you choose to include.
  • App preferences: You may choose settings such as calculation preferences, notification preferences, selected mosque, Quran reading progress, saved tools, or similar app choices.
  • AI prompt content: If you use AI-assisted guidance features, the text you submit may be processed to provide a response.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, app version, language, region, and similar technical information.
  • Usage information: App interactions, feature usage, session information, and general performance data.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs, error reports, and performance information used to improve stability.
  • Subscription status: If the App offers paid features, we may receive subscription status and transaction confirmation information from Apple or subscription management providers. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

1.3 Location Information

Safa may request access to your location to provide features such as prayer times, Qibla direction, nearby mosque information, and location-based reminders. Depending on your device permissions and app settings, location may be obtained from your device's GPS, approximate location, or manually selected city/location.

You can control location access through your device settings. If you deny or disable location access, some features may not work correctly or may require manual location entry.

1.4 Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App may use your device notification permission to send prayer reminders, Jumuah reminders, Ramadan-related reminders, or other app notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide prayer times, Qibla direction, Quran progress, dhikr, Ramadan, Jumuah, and other app features
  • To personalize app settings, reminders, selected locations, and user preferences
  • To process AI-assisted prompts and provide relevant responses
  • To manage subscriptions, premium access, and in-app purchases
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To analyze app performance, detect crashes, fix bugs, and improve the user experience
  • To protect the App, prevent misuse, enforce our terms, and comply with legal obligations

3. AI-Assisted Features

Safa may include AI-assisted features for prompts, reflection, and general guidance. When you use these features, your prompt text may be sent to a third-party AI service provider to generate a response.

Please do not submit sensitive personal information, confidential information, financial information, medical information, legal information, or information about others into AI prompt fields. AI-generated responses may be inaccurate or incomplete and should not be treated as religious rulings, legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or professional counseling.
